Landscape Design Services for the Coachella Valley

Bruce Raymond Design, Inc. is an innovative design team with a passion for desert landscaping. Bruce has over 14 years experience working in the Coachella Valley on new homes, remodels, commercial properties, and HOA communties. We also work with Contractors, Builders, Realtors, and Dreamers to meet their goals and bring their vision to life. From luxury pools to lawn removal, we can offer a great outdoor solution to anything you are looking for!
